## WHY REPLACEMENT FRAMING FAILS

1. **Fear drives avoidance, not engagement** — Workers who fear replacement are 27% more likely to leave their employer (BCG). They're not trying AI tools — they're avoiding them.
2. **Fear creates ethical risk** — EY research: employees who fear AI substitution show higher rates of interpersonal and organizational deviant behavior.
3. **Fear suppresses the exact behavior AI tools need** — AI tools require users to iterate, explore, and experiment. Fear of judgment ("am I just letting AI do my job?") makes users treat AI outputs as final rather than starting points.
4. **Fear is highest in your most valuable users** — Gen Z (52%) fears replacement more than any other generation — yet Gen Z is also the most likely to enroll in AI upskilling courses.
5. **Fear is a top-down problem** — 75% of CEOs fear AI could unseat them, while simultaneously deploying AI aggressively. The anxiety at the top leaks into messaging and culture.
