# Anthropic Labor Market Study — Key Intelligence

## Source
Anthropic: "Labor market impacts of AI: A new measure and early evidence" (April 2026)

## Key Findings

### Jobs Already Being Replaced (Measured, Not Predicted)
- Computer programmers: 74.5% AI exposure
- Customer service reps: 70.1%
- Data entry: 67.1%
- Medical records specialists: 66.7%
- Market research analysts: 64.8%
- Marketing specialists: 64.8%

### The Counter-Intuitive Demographics
- Most exposed workers: OLDER, MORE EDUCATED, earn 47% MORE than average
- NOT warehouse workers or truck drivers — it's lawyers, financial analysts, market researchers, software developers
- More likely to hold graduate degrees

### Entry-Level Pipeline Is Broken
- 14% decline in job-finding for workers aged 22-25 in highly exposed occupations since ChatGPT launched
- The junior-to-senior career path is evaporating
- No comparable effect for workers over 25

### The 30% Nobody Is Talking About
- 30% of American workers have ZERO AI exposure
- Cooks, mechanics, bartenders, dishwashers — completely untouched
- The divide is no longer high skill vs low skill. It's presence vs absence of AI exposure.

### The Gap That Matters
- AI theoretically capable of: 94% of computer/math tasks, 90% of office/admin tasks
- AI currently handling: 33% of computer/math tasks, 40% of office/admin tasks
- Gap is enormous and growing
