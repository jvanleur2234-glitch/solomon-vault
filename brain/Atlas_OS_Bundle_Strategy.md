# Strategic Bundle: Atlas OS + Solomon OS

*Added April 20, 2026*

## The Opportunity

Atlas OS (20.3K GitHub stars, GPL-3.0) is a Windows optimization layer — removes bloat, boosts performance. NOT a standalone OS, can't be forked. BUT the business model and positioning are exactly what we need to copy for Solomon OS.

**Bundle idea:** "Get a clean Windows (Atlas) with all your AI employees pre-installed and running 24/7 (Solomon OS)."

## Atlas OS Business Model

| Tier | Price | What You Get |
|------|-------|-------------|
| Free | $0 | Clean Windows, faster PC, no bloat |
| Pro | $ | Custom themes, advanced tweaks, support |
| Enterprise | custom | Team management, volume licensing |

**Why it works:** Free base gives millions of downloads → brand awareness → paid upgrades.

## Solomon OS Equivalents

| Tier | Price | What You Get |
|------|-------|-------------|
| Solo | $0/mo | 1 AI worker, basic skills, self-hosted |
| Business | $99/mo | 5 AI workers, full skill library, team management, SOC 2 ready |
| Enterprise | custom | Unlimited workers, white-label, dedicated support, compliance suite |

## Key Lessons from Atlas

1. **Clear comparison table** — "Atlas vs vanilla Windows" = "Solomon OS vs doing it manually"
2. **Benchmark social proof** — "Your business runs 10x faster with AI employees"
3. **Free entry point** — Solomon Solo tier gets people in the door
4. **Upgrade path** — Natural growth from solo → business → enterprise
5. **Community-driven** — Atlas has massive Discord/community; we build JackConnect community

## Bundle Architecture

```
Customer buys Atlas OS (clean Windows)
        ↓
We pre-install Solomon OS during Atlas setup
        ↓
Solomon OS auto-starts on boot
        ↓
User creates account → gets 1 free AI worker
        ↓
Upsell to Business tier → 5 AI workers + JackConnect
```

## Competitive Messaging

Atlas says: "Replace your slow Windows with a clean, fast system."

We say: "Replace your manual business operations with AI employees who work 24/7 while you sleep."

The framing: **"Running your business manually is the new Windows 10."**

## Implementation

- **Atlas integration:** When user installs Atlas, we inject Solomon OS as a service that auto-starts
- **Co-marketing:** Atlas users = our target market (tech-savvy, want performance)
- **Revenue split:** Atlas gets affiliate cut when Solomon Business tier sold through Atlas community
