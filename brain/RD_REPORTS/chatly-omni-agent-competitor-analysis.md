# RD Report: Chatly Omni Agent — B2C Competitor Analysis

**Date:** 2026-05-02
**Type:** Competitor
**Source:** https://x.com/chatlyhq/status/2050606347721298380

## What It Is
Chatly launched "Omni Agent" — an all-in-one AI workspace. 900+ likes, 3M views on launch day. Three tiers: Think, Pro, Ultra. Features: deep research, multimedia generation, native workspace integrations, memory layer.

## Pricing
- **Free tier:** EMPTY — Jason Kneen called it out: "free plan with zero features"
- **Think/Pro/Ultra:** Paid tiers (specific prices not visible in scraped data)
- **Chatly base:** $7.50/month for the base product (AI chat + docs + images)

## Competitor Positioning
Chatly = B2C all-in-one content generator. Individual professionals buying AI tools. NOT B2B AI employees.

JCPaid = Sells AI EMPLOYEES to businesses at $299/mo. Each client gets their own agent, their own memory, their own desktop (holaOS). The buyer is a business owner, not an individual power user.

## Threat Assessment
**🟢 Low threat to JCPaid.** Different market segment. Chatly competes with Jasper, Copy.ai, and similar SaaS content tools. Not with AI staffing agencies.

## Watch Conditions
If Chatly launches an enterprise tier with multi-agent orchestration and white-label deployment → re-evaluate. But right now they're a consumer content app.
