## What it is
A Claude-based multi-agent framework for structured decision-making. Emphasizes perspectives over quick answers, seeking unity through discernment rather than consensus by debate. Three core skills: discernment (internal multi-voice), clearness (multi-agent committee), and gathered (participatory).

- deliberation:discernment — internal multiple voices that surface tensions and guide toward unity
- deliberation:clearness — parallel deep analysis committee with clerk synthesis
- deliberation:gathered — participatory where user shapes synthesis
- Grounded in contemplative decision-making principles
- Test-driven development for documentation
