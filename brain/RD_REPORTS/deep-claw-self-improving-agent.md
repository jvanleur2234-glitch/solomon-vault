## What It Does

Dream Cycle framework for autonomous AI agents to self-improve without human input. Two modes:

- **Nightly Mode:** Scans research literature, open-source tools. Scores by relevance. Extracts top items deeply.
- **Weekly Mode:** Reflects on agent performance. Identifies high-leverage improvements. Stages formal proposals with rollback plans.

**Key Concepts:**
- Evidence-based self-improvement (citations, not vibes)
- Governance: separates autonomous vs human-review-required changes
- Proposals include hypotheses and measurable milestones
- Falsifiable change proposals with rollback mechanisms
