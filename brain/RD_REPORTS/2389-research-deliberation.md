## What It Is
Claude-based multi-agent decision support framework that prioritizes perspectives over quick answers. Seeks unity through discernment rather than debate.

## Key Features
- **deliberate:discernment:** Internal voices present diverse viewpoints, surface tensions, guide toward cohesive path
- **deliberate:clearness:** Multi-agent committee for parallel, in-depth analysis with synthesized consensus
- **deliberate:gathered:** Participatory discernment where user + agent voices collaborate

## Principles
- Sense of the meeting (seek unity, not votes)
- Speaking once (each perspective contributes once)
- Deliberate silence
- Standing aside
- Limited blocking to preserve core principles

## Solomon OS Fit
**SKILL** — Multi-agent deliberation for Solomon Browser complex decisions. The "seek unity over votes" principle is powerful — avoids majority tyranny in agent governance. Skills-based implementation means easy Hermes integration. Could be a Solomon OS governance module.

## Differentiation from Quorum
- Quorum: 7-phase formal process, multi-provider
- Deliberation: Quaker-inspired contemplative process, Claude-native
- Both valuable; Quorum for formal votes, Deliberation for ethical/architecture decisions
