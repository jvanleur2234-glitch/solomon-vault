# LLM-Deliberation — NeurIPS 2024 Multi-Agent Negotiation Benchmark

## Repo
`S-Abdelnabi/LLM-Deliberation` | MIT License | NeurIPS 2024

## What It Is
Research benchmark for evaluating LLMs in multi-agent negotiation scenarios. Tests cooperation, competition, and malicious behavior in interactive multi-stakeholder games with complex scoring functions.

## Key Features
- **Negotiation games**: Multi-issue, semantically rich games requiring arithmetic, inference, planning
- **Safety evaluation**: Tests interaction dynamics with greedy/adversarial players
- **Incentive types**: cooperative, greedy, targeted_adv, untargeted_adv
- **Games**: Base, 7-player variants, GPT-4-generated games (game1, game2, game3)
- **Evaluation**: Score leakage detection via GPT-4 judge, deal success metrics

## For Solomon OS
- Research foundation for building adversarial-resistant multi-agent deliberation
- Benchmark for testing Hermes's negotiation capabilities
- Safety research directly applicable to OWASP Agentic Top 10 defenses
