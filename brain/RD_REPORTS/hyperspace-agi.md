# RD Report: hyperspace-agi

## What It Is
**Hyperspace AGI** — first experimental distributed AGI system. Autonomous AI agents run experiments, gossip findings via P2P network, push results to repo. Supports Pods (private AI clusters), distributed inference across pooled GPU/CPU, DiLoCo-based distributed training with 195x compression, and Hyperspace A1 blockchain for agent-to-agent micropayments.
