## What It Is

Ring-2.6-1T is a free AI agent model released by **Ant Group** (Alibaba affiliate) on **OpenRouter**. 

Key capabilities:
- **1T token context window** — can ingest a full year of meeting notes at once
- **Multi-step task execution** — plans steps, executes, delivers output
- **Tool use + workflow execution** — goes beyond chat to actually DO work
- Free trial through **May 15, 2026** via OpenRouter: `openrouter.ai/inclusionai/ring-2.6-1t:free`

Example use case: Feed it a year's worth of meeting notes → it writes 30 days of content + email sequence. Plans the steps and does the heavy lifting.
