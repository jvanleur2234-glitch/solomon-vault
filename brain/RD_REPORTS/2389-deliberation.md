## What It Is
A suite of deliberation skills for AI-assisted decision-making, inspired by Quaker business practice. Three skill variants: `discernment` (internal voices), `clearness` (multi-agent committee), `gathered` (user-participatory). Principles: sense of the meeting, speaking once, intentional silence, standing aside.

---

## Key Capabilities
- `deliberation:discernment` — Internal voices seek clarity on weighty questions
- `deliberation:clearness` — Multi-agent committee for deep parallel analysis
- `deliberation:gathered` — Participatory with user shaping synthesis
- TDD-developed against documented failure modes
- Contemplative traditions applied to AI decision-making

---

## Why It Matters
Most agent frameworks optimize for speed. This framework optimizes for quality of decision on high-stakes questions. The "sense of the meeting" approach surfaces tensions rather than papering over them.
