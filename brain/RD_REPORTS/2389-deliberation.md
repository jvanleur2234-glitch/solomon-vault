## What it is
Claude skills-based multi-agent deliberation framework for consensus-aware decision support. Three skills: `discernment` (internal voices), `clearness` (multi-agent committee), `gathered` (participatory with user). Built on contemplative decision-making principles.

## Key Features
- **3 deliberation skills**: discernment, clearness, gathered
- **Consensus-based**: seeks unity through discernment, not majority votes
- **Claude Skills native**: works as SKILL.md-based skill system
- **Principled process**: sense-of-meeting, speaking once, valuing silence, rare blocking
- **File-based state**: readable markdown/JSON outputs
