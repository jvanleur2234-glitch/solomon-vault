## What It Is
Framework for autonomous AI agents to self-improve via structured nightly scan, synthesis, reflection, and autonomous—but human-checked—implementation.

## Key Capabilities
- Nightly Mode: scans research landscape (papers, open-source, discussions), scores by relevance, deep-dives top items
- Weekly Mode: reflects on performance, identifies high-leverage improvements, stages formal proposals
- Self-improvement loop: hypotheses, rollback plans, measured evidence-backed changes
- Anti-narcissism safeguards and automatic rollback
- Evidence-grounded assessments with citations

## Relevance to Hermes/Solomon
- Nightly scanning pattern could automate Hermes's competitive intelligence gathering
- Self-improvement with rollback aligns with safe autonomous capability growth goals
- Evidence-grounded approach prevents "vibes-based" self-modification
