Opinionated curated reading list covering the evolution of deliberative prompting from 2021 (CoT origins) through DeepSeek R1 (2025). Comprehensive survey of techniques, frameworks, datasets, and tools for LLM reasoning.

- **Tools & Frameworks**: DSPy, LMQL, guidance, LangChain, promptbench
- **Datasets**: ORCA, OpenOrca, Dolphin, CoT Collection, ThoughtSource
