Enterprise-grade LLM vulnerability scanner and AI red-teaming framework. Implements OWASP Top 10 for LLMs with multimodal attacks, multi-step jailbreaks, RL-based adaptive probes, and CI integration.

- **RL-based attacks**: reinforcement learning probes that evolve with defenses
- **Fuzzing**: randomized/edge-case stress testing
- **API stress testing**: validates resilience under load
- **CI-ready**: default config generation, CSV-driven datasets
