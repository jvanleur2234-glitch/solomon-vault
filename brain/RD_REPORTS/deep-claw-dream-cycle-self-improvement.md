## What it does
The Dream Cycle is a self-improvement framework for autonomous AI agents, designed to run continuously with nightly scanning, weekly reflection, and autonomous but human-reviewed self-modification.

Core workflow:
1. **Scanning** — agent surveys research papers, open-source tools, and community discussions, scoring items by relevance
2. **Synthesis** — distills top findings into actionable patterns, capability gaps, and potential improvements
3. **Reflection** — conducts citation-backed assessments of its own performance
4. **Proposal** — generates concrete, falsifiable improvement plans with hypotheses, rollback strategies, and milestone dates
5. **Governance** — distinguishes changes the agent can autonomously implement from those needing human oversight

Two modes:
- Nightly Mode: broad landscape scanning and deep extraction for top items
- Weekly Mode: structured self-reflection to identify high-leverage improvements and formal proposals
