## What It Is
Structured framework for autonomous agent self-improvement via "Dream Cycle" (nightly + weekly modes). Nightly: scan research landscape, score relevance, deep-extract top items. Weekly: structured self-reflection with citation-backed assessments, propose falsifiable improvements with rollback plans. Governance layer defines what agent can change autonomously vs. what requires human approval.

## Why It Matters for Solomon OS
- **Structured self-improvement** — directly addresses Hermes skill self-evolution
- **Citation-backed self-assessment** — no "vibes" — requires evidence for changes
- **Governance before autonomy** — defines what can change unilaterally, preventing unsafe self-modification
- **Production-grade** — built over a month, running in production for several weeks
- **Rollback design** — every improvement is a hypothesis with quality score tracking
