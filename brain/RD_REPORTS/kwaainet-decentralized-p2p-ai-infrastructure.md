## What It Does

Rust-first, decentralized AI infrastructure where users own compute, storage, and data. P2P network with:
- **Decentralized Trust Graph (DTG)** — W3C Verifiable Credentials + did:peer for trust management
- **Persistent node identity** — Ed25519 keypair, constant PeerId across restarts
- **Rust native CLI** — `kwaainet` for deployment and operation
- Cross-platform: macOS (Intel/Apple Silicon), Linux, Windows
