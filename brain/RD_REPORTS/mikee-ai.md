# Mikee.ai Research Report
**Date:** 2026-05-19
**Source:** https://mikee.ai/
**Type:** Competitor / Tool Research

## What It Is
10 free MIT-licensed open-source AI agents, each covering one marketing channel. Self-hosted locally. No SaaS bill. Each agent ships with a free course.

## Key Agents
- **10k-engine** — 5-agent system (offer-finder, outbound, personalization, proposal, objection-handler) for $10k/mo service agency
- **Cold Pitch Boss** — Autonomous freelance client acquisition, Gmail drafts, ~$1 per 30 pitches
- **AI SEO Autopilot** — 4 local agents, ranks in ChatGPT/Perplexity/Google, replaces $250+/mo SaaS
- **10-Day Ads** — Meta ads operator with WhatsApp lead qualification
- **Reddit Sniper, List Building, Affiliate Operator, Ad Alchemist, Conversational Hypnosis**

## Distribution
- Not on GitHub publicly — distributed via subdomain downloads
- Direct download URLs: coldpitch.mikee.ai/download, 10k.mikee.ai/download
- Install via curl pipeline or zip download + Claude self-install

## Business Model
- Free agents as front door to paid agency services (agency.mikee.ai)
- Paid done-for-you builds via the agency
- Agents are battle-tested, used in real agency operations

## How to Make Money
1. USE: Deploy Cold Pitch Boss → land freelance clients → bill $500-5k/mo
2. RESELL: Bundle 10k-engine + Cold Pitch Boss → "$997/mo AI agency-in-a-box" for local businesses
3. WHITE-LABEL: Repurpose free courses as coaching content

## Competitive Analysis
- **JCPaid** = business AI OS with memory, personas, orchestration
- **Mikee** = tactical channel-specific agents with free courses
- DIFFERENT AUDIENCE: Mikee targets developers/freelancers comfortable with CLI
- JCPaid wins on accessibility and no-code positioning
- Mikee wins on tactical depth and specific channel playbooks

## Integration Potential
- Clone 10k-engine → study the 5-agent orchestration pattern for JCPaid Bus improvements
- Clone Cold Pitch Boss → offer as a JCPaid channel-specific skill
- Mikee courses are high-quality training content worth studying
