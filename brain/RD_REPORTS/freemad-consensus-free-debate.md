## What It Is
Production-ready Python implementation of Free-MAD (Consensus-Free Multi-Agent Debate) algorithm. Eliminates need for consensus, evaluates entire debate process (journey) rather than just final vote. Two runtimes: debate (standard) and autonomous (persistent tasks with quorum review).

## Key Features
- Consensus-free: avoids conformity bias and majority tyranny
- Evaluates entire debate journey, not just outcome
- Autonomous runtime: persistent, resumable tasks with policy bounds
- Live event streaming + dashboard
- Production-ready implementation

## Solomon OS Fit
**Deliberation pillar.** Free-MAD's consensus-free approach to multi-agent debate is novel. Its emphasis on evaluating the process (not just outcome) aligns with Solomon's self-improvement philosophy — agents should learn from HOW they debated, not just what they concluded.
