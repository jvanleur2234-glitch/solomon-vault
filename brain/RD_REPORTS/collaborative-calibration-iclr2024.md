# Collaborative Calibration — ICLR 2024 Multi-Agent Deliberation

## Repo
`minnesotanlp/collaborative-calibration` | MIT License | ICLR 2024 R2-FM Workshop

## What It Is
Research on improving LLM confidence estimation via multi-agent deliberation. Simulates groups of LLMs deliberating to calibrate confidence and produce better justifications.

## Key Features
- **Deliberation simulator**: Multi-agent groups debating to reach calibrated consensus
- **Confidence calibration**: Reduces over/under-confidence in LLM outputs
- **Ensemble modes**: Both model ensemble and agent ensemble support
- **Benchmark datasets**: Preprocessed evaluation data included

## For Solomon OS

- Rationalization research applicable to Hermes's reasoning transparency
