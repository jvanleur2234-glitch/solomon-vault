## What It Is

Sauna.ai is a multiplayer AI agent platform with three core features:
1. **Personal AI Brain** — 3,800+ tools connected, state-of-the-art memory, skills/schedules, AI-first CRM
2. **GUI for commanding 50 agents** — Live sessions panel, work queue with sign-off, "game mode" to clear the queue
3. **Multiplayer mode** — Brain sharing between team members (read-only or full access), communal saunas with permissioning

**Stack:** Claude, GPT-5, Gemini, GLM-5.1, DeepSeek, Kimi — user can pick based on budget.

**Pricing:** Launch deal — first 2,000 people use code `LAUNCH` for $80 of weekly credits.

| Feature | Sauna.ai | Solomon OS |

| Multi-agent coordination | ✅ 50+ with GUI | ✅ Solomon Bus (text-based) |

**Key gap we've identified:** Solomon OS has the intelligence (RENU API, Hermes skills, Russell Tuna) but lacks a unified GUI for commanding multiple agents simultaneously. Sauna.ai shows the market wants this.
