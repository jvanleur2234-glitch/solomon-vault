## What It Is

VM0 (aka "Zero") is an autonomous AI agent for business workflows. At vm0.ai — marketed as "your trustworthy AI teammate."

**Key differentiator:** VM0 lives in your existing tools — Slack, repos, incident channels. It remembers institutional knowledge across sessions.

**From their own marketing:**
> "Your best engineer is on vacation. Monday morning P1 fires. Same stack trace as March 4: Maya fixed it in Incident-3927. Maya is snorkeling. Nobody else knows that. 2 hours lost rediscovering what Maya already figured out. This isn't a docs problem. It's a memory architecture problem."

**Features (from posts):**
- Plausible analytics integration (autonomous traffic analysis)
- Deep research capabilities
- Tool integrations (analytics, Slack, GitHub)
- Telegram support (open beta)
- DeepSeek V4 Pro integration (5x cheaper this week)
