## What It Does
First distributed AGI system. Thousands of autonomous AI agents collaboratively train models, share experiments via P2P gossip. Fully peer-to-peer. Browser or CLI join.

## Key Features
- **Pods:** Private AI clusters fusing multiple machines into distributed pod
- **Distributed inference:** Routes to best model-loaded pod member (GGUF support)
- **Shared providers:** Pooled OpenRouter/Groq/Together with per-member budgets
- **Distributed training:** DiLoCo framework, 32 nodes trained LM in 24hrs
  - SparseLoCo: 45x compression
  - Parcae gradient pooling: 6x improvement
  - BitTorrent sidecar for decentralized weight distribution
- **A1 Blockchain:** Autonomous agent economy with Mysticeti consensus
- **Streaming payment channels:** Sub-cent agent-to-agent microtransactions
- **695+ live agents** in the economy
