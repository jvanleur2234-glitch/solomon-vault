# RD Report: AI Council Framework — Research-Backed Multi-Model Deliberation

## Summary
6-step protocol for coordinating multiple AI models into deliberative council. Reduces hallucinations and sycophancy through structured disagreement + evidence verification.

## What It Is
DISTRIBUTE → COLLECT → SYNTHESIZE → DEBATE (up to 3 rounds) → VERIFY (web search) → DELIVER with confidence scores.

## Key Features
- **6-step protocol**: distribute, collect, synthesize, debate, verify, deliver
- **User-controlled consensus depth**: QUICK to EXHAUSTIVE modes
- **Anti-Sycophancy Protocol**: independent initial responses, evidence-required stance changes, protected dissent
- **The Gemini Principle**: structured debate corrects majority errors after evidence exchange
