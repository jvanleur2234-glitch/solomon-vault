# THE AIRLINE NOT RAILROAD PLAN
**Solomon OS — Decentralized AI Infrastructure**
*Created: 2026-04-15*

---

## THE MISSION
Build AI infrastructure that nobody controls, everybody can use, and anyone can profit from.

**Railroad:** One company owns the tracks. You pay to use them. They decide who rides.
**Airline:** Many companies own planes. You choose who flies you. Competition keeps prices low, service high.

---

## PLAN — 3 LAYERS, NO BLOCKCHAIN UNTIL YEAR 2

### LAYER 1: THE PRIVACY LAYER (Year 1 — No Coin)
**Goal:** Make privacy the default, not a feature.

**What we build:**
- Local AI inference engine that runs on smallest hardware (Ollama + fine-tuned distro)
- Zero-data-leaving device — every query answered locally unless user explicitly approves cloud
- "Solomon Air" app — simple enough for grandma. One button: "Ask." Camera, voice, text.
- Federated learning — model learns from your device without your data going anywhere
- Proof layer — you CAN prove you contributed quality data without revealing the data

**Revenue:**
- Freemium: 50 free queries/day, $5/mo unlimited
- Premium: voice, camera, file analysis

**Why this first:** Prove people will pay for privacy. No blockchain needed yet.

---

### LAYER 2: THE NETWORK LAYER (Year 1.5 — Bittensor Subnet)
**Goal:** Incentivize compute contributors without building new blockchain.

**What we build:**
- Deploy a NEW subnet on Bittensor (existing chain, 80K+ holders, live trading)
- Compute providers download "Solomon Node" software
- Node earns TAO token for verified inference
- Anyone with a GPU can join — gaming PCs to data centers
- Staking + validator network verifies work happened (Bittensor's existing mechanism)

**Revenue:**
- Network takes 5% of TAO rewards
- 20% of TAO goes to Solomon Treasury
- Treasury funds development + royalty payouts

**Why Bittensor:** Proven blockchain. Existing liquidity. No custom token needed yet.

---

### LAYER 3: THE ROYALTY LAYER (Year 2 — Smart Contract)
**Goal:** Pay people for ideas, not just compute.

**What we build:**
- "Idea Submission" contract on Bittensor
- User submits feature request, skill idea, or data contribution
- Community validators rate quality (staked voting)
- Top ideas implemented by open-source dev community
- Contributors earn ongoing royalty in TAO every time their skill is used

**Royalty split:**
- 60% to idea creator
- 25% to implementing developer
- 15% to Solomon Treasury

**Why this works:** Open source projects die because contributors get nothing. This pays them forever.

## HOW WE KEEP IT OPEN FOREVER

**Legal layer:**
- Core protocol: AGPL license (copyleft — any modification must be open)
- Trademark held by non-profit foundation, not a company
- No patents on core IP
- Board: elected by token holders, must be 51% independent

**Technical layer:**
- No API keys required to build on top
- All data formats open standard
- No single point of failure — runs on thousands of independent nodes

## WHAT WE HAVEN'T THOUGHT OF (SPOTTED GAPS)

1. **AI Will** — Make a Will for your AI self. Who inherits your model when you die? Your AI persona, trained on your data, passes to family. Nobody else has this.

2. **AI Divorce** — Split AI assets fairly when relationships end. Custody of trained models, conversation history, custom skills.

3. **AI Credit Score** — Prove you're a good AI citizen. Quality contributions unlock better rates on inference, higher royalty splits. Gamifies good behavior.

4. **Dead Man's Switch** — If you stop using the network for X months, your model gets shared with researchers. Your data lives on helping people, not sitting in a server forever.

5. **Cross-Border AI** — Like a VPN for AI. Route your query through nodes in different countries to satisfy data residency laws. Nodes earn extra for serving regulated industries.
