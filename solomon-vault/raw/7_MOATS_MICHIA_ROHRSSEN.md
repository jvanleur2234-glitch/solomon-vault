# The 7 Moats for Solomon OS

**Source:** Michia Rohrssen — "The only type of business I'd build in 2026"
**Applied to:** Solomon OS (AI staffing / agent platform for SMBs)

---

## MOAT 1: BRAND MOAT
**Definition:** Your product becomes associated with desirable traits your target audience wants in their lives.

**Solomon OS application:**
- "Solomon OS = the AI that actually works for your business, not against it"
- "Russell Tuna = your tireless AI employee who never sleeps, never quits, never costs benefits"
- Build the feeling of having an elite AI team that's reliable, always-on, genuinely helpful
- Warren Buffett invested in brand businesses for decades — brand is the ultimate moat
- **Action:** Start with "AI staffing" brand, own the category. When people think "AI worker for my business" → Solomon OS

---

## MOAT 2: DISTRIBUTION MOAT
**Definition:** You have an unfair advantage in getting your product in front of people.

**Solomon OS application:**
- Telegram is already distribution — Jack already has Russell Tuna bot
- Every existing Solomon OS user becomes a distribution channel (refer friends)
- The affiliate program (30% recurring) turns every customer into a sales rep
- "Super Solomon" referral network effect: each new AI employee user brings 2-3 more
- **Action:** Double down on Telegram as the primary distribution channel. It's already the app every real estate agent lives in

---

## MOAT 3: DATA MOAT
**Definition:** You have proprietary data that improves your product as you get more data.

**Solomon OS application:**
- Every task Jack does → learning what works for his business
- Watch-once engine learns workflows: what tools does a real estate agent actually use?
- This data compounds. A new competitor has zero Watch-once data for real estate
- The more agents deployed, the smarter the AI team gets across ALL clients
- **Action:** Build the data flywheel from day 1. Track every workflow, every decision, every task. This is the "AI learns your business" moat no one else can copy

---

## MOAT 4: ECOSYSTEM MOAT
**Definition:** Your product becomes a platform everyone else is building on or around.

**Solomon OS application:**
- Every Hermes skill is a plugin to Solomon OS
- Every agent that connects to Solomon OS (Russell Tuna, JackConnect, external agents) = ecosystem grows
- Once customers have 5-10 agents running on Solomon OS, they're locked in
- "I'm running my entire AI team through Solomon OS" = ecosystem lock-in
- **Action:** Make Solomon OS the hub that all AI tools plug into. Every integration = more ecosystem lock-in

---

## MOAT 5: COMPLIANCE / LEGAL MOAT
**Definition:** You've gone through regulatory or legal processes that others can't shortcut.

**Solomon OS application:**
- Real estate has strict compliance (state licenses, fair housing laws, MLS rules)
- HIPAA compliance for medical businesses
- Financial advice regulations for investment agents
- Each vertical has compliance barriers that take time + money to navigate
- **Action:** Build compliance checklists INTO the agents. "Jack's AI knows fair housing rules" = barrier competitors can't copy with AI alone

---

## MOAT 6: HARD-CODED IP MOAT (Michia's own moat)
**Definition:** You've built proprietary logic in software that handles complex real-world systems.

**Solomon OS application:**
- Watch-once automation engine: complex real estate workflows encoded into AI agents
- The " Superintendent RE" system = years of knowledge encoded into code
- Each vertical (real estate, medical, legal) has unique workflows that take time to understand
- You can't replicate "knows how a real estate deal actually closes" in weeks
- **Action:** Build deep vertical-specific knowledge. The longer you work in a vertical, the more encoded IP you have that competitors can't shortcut

---

## THE 7TH MOAT (What Michia MISSED):

## MOAT 7: RELATIONSHIP / TRUST MOAT
**Definition:** Your customers trust YOU specifically, not just your product.

**Why this is the ultimate moat:**
- In SMB world, people buy from people they trust
- Jack doesn't just trust "an AI" — he trusts Solomon OS because he trusts Joseph
- Every time Joseph delivers on a promise, the trust moat grows
- Competitors can copy the product, but they can't copy 5 years of trust
- Warren Buffett doesn't just own Coke's brand — he owns the relationship with shareholders

**Solomon OS application:**
- "Built by Joseph Vanleur, for small business owners who want real AI employees"
- The personal touch: "I built this for Jack because I know what he needs"
- Every testimonial, every case study, every "it works for MY business" = trust compound
- **Action:** Joseph's personal reputation is a moat. Every SMB owner who knows Joseph = warm lead. The family + friends network = first customers.
