## What It Is
OpenScreen is a free, open-source screen recording tool — an alternative to Screen Studio. Records specific windows or full screen, captures mic + system audio, supports auto/manual zoom/pan with motion blur, annotations (text/arrows/images), trimming, speed adjustments, and exports in multiple aspect ratios (16:9, 9:16, 1:1, 4:5, 4:3). MIT license, no watermarks, free for commercial use.
