## What It Is

Peer-to-peer protocol for decentralized AI inference using 1-bit models. 70-82% more energy efficient than GPU-based systems, runs on any CPU. Blockchain provenance, consent-based resource usage. OpenAI-compatible API.

## Key Capabilities

- **1-bit model inference**: CPU-efficient, no GPU required
- **70-82% energy savings** vs GPU systems
- **P2P network**: WebSocket-based distributed inference
- **Consent-based**: No resource used without permission
- **Blockchain provenance**: Immutable inference records
- **OpenAI-compatible API**: Drop-in replacement
- **8+ organizations** producing 1-bit models (Microsoft, TII, MBZUAI, ETH Zurich)
