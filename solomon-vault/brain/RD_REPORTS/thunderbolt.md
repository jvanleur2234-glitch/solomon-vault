# R&D Report: Thunderbolt

**Date:** April 19, 2026

## What It Is

Thunderbolt is Thunderbird's AI chat client — cross-platform (web, iOS, Android, Mac, Linux, Windows), local/offline models, enterprise on-prem deployment.

## Key Details

- **Repo:** github.com/thunderbird/thunderbolt
- **Stars:** 2.2K
- **License:** Mozilla Public License 2.0
- **Stack:** Tauri (Rust backend), TypeScript frontend, Drizzle ORM, Ollama/llama.cpp compatible
- **Forked:** jvanleur2234-glitch/thunderbolt ✅

## Pitch

"AI You Control: Choose your models. Own your data. Eliminate vendor lock-in."

## Why It Matters for JCPaid

Thunderbird has MASSIVE distribution — it's pre-installed on Linux distros, used by millions. This could be the Thunderbird AI integration layer for JCPaid.
