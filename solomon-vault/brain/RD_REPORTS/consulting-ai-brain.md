# RD Report: AI Brain for Consulting Firms

## What It Is
Nik Fuller (30-person consulting firm) designed a 3-layer "Brain" system for consulting firms:

**Problem**: Consulting firms run on SharePoint they don't actually use. Institutional knowledge lives in Teams threads, individual OneDrives, call transcripts, CRM notes. No one captures it. New hires take 6 months to ramp. Sales can't answer "have we done this before?"

**Data governance crisis**: Client data on individual laptops with no audit trail = liability as regulations tighten.

## The Three Layers

### Layer 1: Index Everything
- AI search platform connecting Teams, SharePoint, call transcripts, CRM
- Native connectors → index builds itself
- No new platforms or behavior changes required

### Layer 2: Per-Practitioner Context
- Every team member gets a personalized brief rebuilt nightly
- Weighted by their specific roles on each active project
- Engagement Manager + Lead Consultant = both delivery + commercial signal

### Layer 3: Action Engine
- Brain doesn't just surface information — it creates and assigns tasks
- Daily directive ranked by urgency
- Flags scope creep before it becomes a crisis
- Human stays in authority; every action is reviewable

### Compounding Moat: Knowledge Extraction at Project Close
- AI extracts anonymized knowledge record when project closes
- Client names stay, personal names stripped
- Preserves: decisions made, integrations built, problems encountered, lessons learned
- After 1 year: 100 closed projects worth of delivery intelligence
- After 3 years: several hundred → plain language queryable corpus
