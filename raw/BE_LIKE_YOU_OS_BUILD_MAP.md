## WHAT YOU'RE DESCRIBING

## THE 4 LAYERS

### Layer 1 — Base OS
Fork LineageOS (Android open source, 100M+ users, completely legal).
- Full Android app compatibility
- Bootable on thousands of phones
- All drivers included
- Don't rebuild from scratch

### Layer 2 — VoIP Phone System
Take vphone-cli's SIP + WebRTC approach, bake in Linphone SDK.
- Every phone gets a free virtual number
- Global calling/texting, zero carrier bills
- **This is the killer feature that makes people switch**

### Layer 3 — Solomon OS Core
Runs as a SYSTEM SERVICE, not an app.
- Intercepts calls, drafts responses, runs automations
- JackConnect becomes default productivity suite
- Hermes becomes system-wide AI router
- Solomon Air manages all cloud sync + decentralized infra
- Every AI skill is a system-level service

### Layer 4 — AI Launcher + Marketplace
Replace the app grid with a chat interface.
- Instead of opening Instagram, you hire an AI agent from the marketplace
- Developers build "Solomon Skills"
- Solomon OS takes 30% cut
- **This is the App Store revenue model but for AI agents**
